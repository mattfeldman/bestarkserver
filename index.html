Rules
Play nice

Have fun - don't spoil others fun
Really, we're all trying to have fun here, so do whatever is fun to you, but please try to be a good neighbor and consider others.
Spread out
Out of respect for others please do not start building close to another tribe's build without their permission
Keep track of your Dinosaurs
Do not leave your tames on wander/aggressive. Please keep your dinos in your base.
No blocking dense resource nodes
Areas like the 'volcano' have dense resources spawns for crystal/obsidian/metal, placing structures near the resource nodes can interfere with the spawn. You can build on the perimeter so you can still be close to the nodes without them from spawning.
No laggy massive bases or dino hordes
Build whatever base and collect whatever dinosaurs you need. We're concerned with excessive dino populations and bases that are known sources of lag on the server.
No building at spires or carno island
These building areas are reserved.
No abusive/hateful speech
We want to ensure the server is a healthy environment for everyone. We're concerned here with public chat, we do not want global filled with private arguments and inflamatory humor.

About

We are a **casual PVE ark server** with beginner friendly rates. The server was spawned out of the fustration of finding a stable PVE centric community. Our goal was to create an open community of regular ark players. The server is currently based in **Seattle, Washington** Our admin philosophy is that we will remove those who are intent on damaging the server, but otherwise do not interfere with personal disputes, tribe ownership, lost dinos, spawning items, etc. We believe this hands off approach fits the friendly community of our server while giving our staff a chance to focus on important issues and a chance to enjoy ark themselves.
